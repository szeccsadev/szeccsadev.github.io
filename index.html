About Me

My name is Kevin Király. I was born on 10th February 2007 in Hungary. I started school in 2014 and I’m still studying. I’m currently in Class 11 in high-school studying as a pastry chef, even if I’m not planning to work after school in this job. My primary language is Hungarian and the secondary is English, I’m learning English since 2014, so big achevement. After school I’m planning to go and work as a BPA (Border Patrol Agent) at the Serbian border of the country, since I’m very close to it, and because the good salary. I do love tech, developing and playing videogames. I make websites in HTML, CSS and JS to close friends or relatives who needs a website for their business, promoting themselves or just for fun. I made this website for myself after using captures from Archive.org for a year. I did a lot of polishing optimalisation on these websites to meet my needs but it was impossible due the outdated code. So I wrote this one for myself. I often go for cycling, running or to the gym to do some workout. So that’s my story.

The mascot; Youhuu

After World Athletics announced the mascot of the World Athletics Championships Budapest 2023 back in May 2023 I immediately saw something in it... It was a bit disappointing to see it in reality for only nine days but these nine days were golden. Since that I still love that thing so much, and miss it of course. I know that I don't have the rights to use it, even for personal use. But I'm not using it to gain any income or for advertising or else... But still... I still have plans with its real-life version... Don't be afraid, I know where to start and what am I doing... See you later.
